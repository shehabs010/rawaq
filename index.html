Who We Are

Architecture
is a Human Act

العمارة فعل إنساني قبل أن تكون هندسة

Riwaq Developments is the natural result of years of experience in real estate planning and development. Our journey began with understanding land, reading place, and envisioning a future that can truly be lived.

At Riwaq, nothing is left to coincidence. Land is defined. Vision is defined. Direction is defined. Every line we draw is a commitment — to people, to place, and to time.

We plan today to draw a future that is more balanced, calmer, and more livable. We place people at the heart of every project, creating spaces that simplify life, enhance comfort, and provide security for families and future generations.

We also believe that development carries a responsibility toward the community — contributing to local growth, strengthening belonging, and improving quality of life for everyone who lives, works, or visits our projects.

For us, planning is a human act
before it is an engineering process.
Our Foundation

What We Stand For

01

Human-Centered Design

03

Community Responsibility

Development carries a duty to the surrounding community. We invest in local growth, belonging, and quality of life for all.

About The Founders

Built on Decades of Real Estate Mastery

Riwaq Developments was established by a recognized name in real estate development and investment with more than two decades of experience, delivering a series of projects that left a lasting impact across various Egyptian locations.

The company was co-founded by a leading entrepreneur in the real estate sector with over ten years of experience, having successfully developed numerous residential, commercial, and service projects.

Riwaq is built upon the combined strength of administrative and engineering expertise, reflected in a portfolio exceeding twenty projects across multiple Egyptian governorates.
